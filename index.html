The Partnership Framework

Five deliberate stages. Each one moves you closer to a collaboration that actually performs.

  • 01
    Discovery
    Understanding your goals, audience, product, and what a meaningful outcome looks like for your brand or creative identity.
  • 02
    Strategic Matching
    Connecting you with the right creators—filtered by audience alignment, content quality, and campaign fit, not just follower count.
  • 03
    Campaign Structuring
    Planning collaborations with clear objectives, defined deliverables, and structured timelines that set both sides up for success.
  • 04
    Execution & Management
    Seamless coordination from briefing to final delivery—we handle the process so both parties can focus on what they do best.
  • 05
    Optimization & Reporting
    Improving results with real data. Every campaign closes with a clear analysis, and every insight informs the next collaboration.

    Costs vary based on creators, campaign scope, and deliverables. We help structure campaigns that align with your budget and objectives.
